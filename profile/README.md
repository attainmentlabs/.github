# Attainment

**AI automation for professional services.** We cut costs and recover revenue.

We build AI systems that handle the repetitive, rules-based work that keeps scaling expensive: phone calls, intake processes, data entry, scheduling. Our clients are service businesses with 10 to 500+ locations, where a 1-hour daily reduction in manual work compounds into millions.

## What We Build

- **AI Receptionists** for healthcare and professional services. Natural conversation, bilingual (EN/FR), integrated with practice management systems. Patients don't experience hold music or phone trees.
- **Process Automation** for operations teams drowning in repetitive workflows. We audit, identify the highest-ROI automation targets, and deploy.
- **Growth Systems** for companies that need marketing infrastructure, not just marketing advice.
